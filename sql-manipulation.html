SQL Project 1: Walmart Sales Data Analysis:

This project explores the Walmart Sales data to understand top-performing branches and products, sales trends of different products, and customer behavior. The goal is to study how sales strategies can be improved and optimized. The dataset was obtained from the Kaggle Walmart Sales Forecasting Competition.

The major aim of this project is to gain insight into the sales data of Walmart to understand the different factors that affect sales across various branches.

The dataset contains sales transactions from three different branches of Walmart, respectively located in Mandalay, Yangon, and Naypyitaw. The data contains 17 columns and 1000 rows:

Column Description Data Type
invoice_id Invoice of the sales made VARCHAR(30)
branch Branch at which sales were made VARCHAR(5)
city The location of the branch VARCHAR(30)
customer_type The type of the customer VARCHAR(30)
gender Gender of the customer making the purchase VARCHAR(10)
product_line Product line of the product sold VARCHAR(100)
unit_price The price of each product DECIMAL(10, 2)
quantity The amount of the product sold INT
VAT The amount of tax on the purchase FLOAT(6, 4)
total The total cost of the purchase DECIMAL(10, 2)
date The date on which the purchase was made DATE
time The time at which the purchase was made TIMESTAMP
payment_method The method of payment used VARCHAR(20)
cogs Cost Of Goods Sold DECIMAL(10, 2)
gross_margin_percentage Gross margin percentage FLOAT(11, 9)
gross_income Gross Income DECIMAL(10, 2)
rating Customer rating FLOAT(2, 1)
Analysis List:
  • Product Analysis: Conduct analysis to understand the different product lines, identify the best-performing products, and find areas needing improvement.
  • Sales Analysis: Analyze sales trends to evaluate the effectiveness of sales strategies and identify necessary modifications.
  • Customer Analysis: Analyze customer segments, purchase trends, and the profitability of each segment.
Approach Used:
  • Data Wrangling: Inspection and cleaning of data to handle NULL and missing values.
  • Build a database, create tables, and insert data.
  • Select columns with null values (if any) and handle them appropriately.

SQL Project 2: Comprehensive SQL-Based Data Analysis for a Music Store:

In this project, I conducted an extensive analysis of a music store's dataset using SQL, focusing on key business metrics and customer behaviors. The project involved solving various query-based challenges, ranging from identifying the senior-most employee to determining the best customers and most profitable cities. Through SQL queries, I extracted insights into customer spending habits, artist popularity, and genre preferences across different countries. The analysis included identifying the top rock music listeners, determining the most popular music genres by country, and evaluating customer expenditures on artists. This project showcases my ability to handle complex data sets, generate actionable insights, and support business decisions using advanced SQL techniques.

The questions in this analysis are designed to uncover critical insights from the music store's dataset through SQL queries. The analysis begins with basic queries, such as identifying the senior-most employee and pinpointing the countries with the highest invoice counts. As the complexity increases, the focus shifts to understanding customer behavior—like finding the best customer and the city with the highest sales, which could be targeted for promotional events.

In the moderate section, the queries delve into customer preferences, such as identifying all Rock music listeners and the top rock bands by track count. The advanced queries challenge deeper analysis, including determining the total amount spent by customers on specific artists, identifying the most popular music genre in each country, and finding the top-spending customers by country.

These questions collectively aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of the music store's operational metrics and customer dynamics, supporting strategic decisions and targeted marketing efforts.
