Credit Card Transactions and Customer Analysis Using SQL and Power BI

In this project, I carried out an in-depth analysis of credit card transaction data and customer information using SQL for data manipulation and Power BI for visualization. The dataset included detailed records of customer demographics, transaction histories, and associated address data. I developed two comprehensive dashboards: one focused on analyzing transaction patterns, including spending behavior and transaction volumes, and another centered on customer demographics and segmentation. SQL was employed to extract, clean, and integrate data from multiple sources, enabling a robust foundation for the visualizations. This analysis provided valuable insights into customer spending patterns, risk factors, and potential areas for targeted marketing efforts. The project demonstrated my ability to leverage data analytics tools to drive business intelligence in the financial services sector.
